About

Dr Ghassan has accumulated vast experience in the orthopaedic field since he graduated in the top 10% from the College of Medicine, University of Baghdad, 1988. He moved on to first work in the NHS at Glasgow in 1994 where he was awarded an FRCS from the Royal College of Physicians and Surgeons of Glasgow. He has spent all 33 years of his career working towards or as an orthopaedic surgeon leaving countless patients and colleagues with nothing but positive remarks about his work.

He has a sub specialist interest in upper limb surgery and has received excellent fellowship level training in shoulder, elbow, wrist and hand surgery from King’s College Hospital, London, and University Hospital of Salzburg, Austria. His eagerness and passion in the medical field drove him to publish multiple world class research papers which can be found listed on his CV. In furthering his contribution to the progression of the medical community, Mr Al-Yassari spent a significant period of his career (2014 – 2019) in developing the orthopaedic training programme in the Sultanate of Oman.

Now a father of four, he enjoys travelling, sightseeing and action films and as a consultant he is responsible for the supervision and mentorship of the specialist trainee doctors at North Wick Park Hospital orthopaedic department.
